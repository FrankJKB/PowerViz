POWER VIZ is a visualization app which displays a daily quote with the possibility to set notifications to be reminded of this quote.

You are the one who chooses the quotes to be displayed, based on your visualization goals and needs.

A daily quote is set for the day, and can't be changed until the following day.

Click on "Add New Quote" to add a quote to your list.

Click on "Show Quote of the Day" to view the quote and add reminders. Reminders can be sent randomly or regularly, depending on your preferences.

Do not close the app if you want to receive the notifications.
